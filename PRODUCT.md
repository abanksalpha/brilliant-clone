# PRODUCT.md

register: product

## Users

A high school student taking AP Physics C: Electricity and Magnetism, or someone
self-studying for the exam, who learns better by doing than by reading. They are
motivated but time-pressed, often working alone at a desk in the evening. They
want to get good at solving AP-level problems, not to be entertained.

## Product purpose

APT(utor) teaches AP Physics C: E&M through explicit instruction and heavy
problem practice. Each lesson is one guided session: a short spaced review, a
quick generative inquiry, a concise explanation, worked examples that fade into
independent practice, and AP-level problems solved by hand and graded for
reasoning. The product serves the work; it is a tool, not a toy.

## Brand and tone

Calm, focused, confidence-building. Hand-drawn on paper: warm neutral surfaces, a
dot-grid backdrop, sketch borders, a pixel display face for headings. The voice
is plain and direct. Every word earns its place. No hype, no filler, no em
dashes.

## Strategic principles

- Explicit instruction first; worked examples before independent solving.
- Difficulty lives in spacing, interleaving, and AP-level problems, never in
  withholding instruction or dumbing problems down.
- The interface shows only what a step needs. No extraneous explanation in the UI.
- One clear action per screen. Progress is obvious; the next step is obvious.

## Anti-references

- Gamified discovery edtech that optimizes streaks and delight over durable
  learning.
- Busy SaaS dashboards: hero-metric headers, identical card grids, dense chrome.
- Anything that reads as generic or AI-generated.
